About Us

We are a Myanmar-based boutique research agency specializing in market intelligence and consumer insights. We believe in the holistic view that the targeted market and consumers’ needs are critical to grow every business sustainably in today’s competitive environment. At Insights Plus, we apply agile and emerging research methods to uncover in-depth insights that will put your business one step ahead of the competition. We take pride in delivering unbiased and independent research findings to our clients.

Why Choose A Boutique Research Agency?

Quality over quantity

We are a team of driven individuals who are experienced in research and marketing. Insights Plus is dedicated to handling a limited number of projects at a time to give our full attention to each client’s needs. This means you as a client can be rest assured that we will deliver the quality research tailored to your business.

Cost-effectiveness

There is a widely-perceived belief that market research is costly. Our aim is to make market research accessible by businesses of any scale - from local SMEs to multi-national corporations. As a boutique research firm, our lean approach puts us in an advantage to offer customized research to our clients with budget-friendly rates without compromising on quality.

Flexibility

As a boutique agency, our lines of communications are abridged. We are able to provide much more bespoke and flexible services to our clients within a short turnaround time. Insights Plus ensures constant communication with our clients throughout the research study.
